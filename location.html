KING’S CROSS

At the centre of the King's Cross development is The Granary Complex - a new cultural focal point and home to the world famous arts college Central Saint Martins. A magnet for shoppers and visitors, Coal Drops Yard offers an extrodinary retail and dining experience, with over fifty stores, retaurants and cafés.

TILEYARD STUDIOS

Located just round the corner from Europe's largest community of independent artists and businesses, The Brewery Building is part of the creative neighbourhood, that surrounds Tileyard London. The area is home to a diverse and bustling community, attracting some of London's most forward-thinking companies and individuals.
