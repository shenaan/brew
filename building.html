THE BUILDING

A striking building in the rapidly developing King's Cross area, offering 11,000 sq ft of contemporary office space.

Designed by Squire & Partners with innovative businesses in mind, the full-height glazing provides the interiors with abundant natural light, creating bright, open space to think and create.

A building to support creative businesses, providing them with space to work and room to grow. A prime opportunity to become part of one of London's fastest growing districts.
